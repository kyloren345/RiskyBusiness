Methamphetamine

Methamphetamine is a potent central nervous system stimulant that is mainly used as a recreational drug and is less used for treatment purposes.

Information

Street Name

The most common street names of meth are; ice, pookie, cookies, no doze, speed, crank, chalk, wash, trash, dunk, gak, cotton candy and much more.

How it can be taken

Meth is typically smoked or inhaled and when snorted or orally ingested produces euphoria.

About Meth

Meth is a stimulant that effects the central nervous system and creates a rush.

Short term effects

Short term effects of taking meth are; increased attention, increased activity, decreased appetite, euphoria rush, increased respiration and rapid/irregular heartbeat.

Long term effects

Long term effects of taking meth are; addiction, psychosis, changes in brain structure and function, thinking and motor skills deficit, memory loss, aggressive behaviour and mood disturbances.
